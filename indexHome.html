Ecological Footprint Survey


Welcome and thank you for your interest in our Ecological survey! Participation in this study is completely voluntary.


Information and Purpose

In our research we aim to investigate the ecological footPrint size of various people on the planet. This has to do with the impact of our regular everyday activities on the planet we live in. Our goal is to measure the ecological footprint sizes of people across gender.


Course of Study

In this study you will be asked to answer questions within the domain of your daily living habits. The questions consists of:

  • Multiple choice questions
  • Checkbox questions
The study will take between 15 and 20 minutes.


Confidentiality and handling of the data

All information collected is completely confidential. Your specific name and identifying information will not be associated with any part of the written report of the research. Resposes to the tasks will not be shared with anyone but the PI.


Voluntary participation

Again your participation in the study is completely voluntary.
