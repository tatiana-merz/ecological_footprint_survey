Welcome and thank you for your interest in our Ecological survey! Participation in this study is completely voluntary.

Information and Purpose

In our research we aim to investigate the ecological footPrint size of various people on the planet with the aim of measuring the ecological footprint between genders.

Course of Study

In this study you will be asked to complete the different tasks on your daily living habits. You will receive more detailed instructions as you continue. The study will take between 15 and 20 minutes.

Advantages

  1. for participants: You get an insight into your ecological footprint. If you are interested, you can proceed to see your footprint size after your participation.
  2. for science: More information about the cumulative ecological footprint size of people distributed across the planet.

Risks and side effects for participants

There are no risks associated with participating in this study.

Insurance coverage

Not required.

Confidentiality and handling of the data

All information collected is completely confidential. Your specific name and identifying information will not be associated with any part of the written report of the research. Resposes to the tasks will not be shared with anyone but the PI.

Voluntary participation

Your participation in the study is completely voluntary.

Possibility of dropping out of the study

You can end your participation in the study at any time and without giving a reason. If you end your participation early, your data will not be saved. Please note that if you leave the study prematurely, it will not be possible to compute your footprint.
